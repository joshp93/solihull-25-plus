Solihull Christadelphian 25+ Fellowship Day

EDIFYING

Bro Tim Cresswell

God willing
  12th November, 10am
  Solihull Christadelphian Hall, Grove Ave, Solihull B91 2AH
£  10 for the day

A day of bible study and fellowship for those 25 and over. Both baptised and non baptised are welcome.

Bro. Tim will be leading a mixture of talks and discussions based on the subject "Edifying". We will focus on how we can build-up ourselves, our friends and our ecclesias.

£10 for the day. Lunch and dinner provided. There will be a range of activities organised by the members of Solihull Ecclesia.

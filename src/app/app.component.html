Solihull Christadelphian 25+ Fellowship Day

"Go from your country, your people and your father's household to the land I will show you"

God willing
  21st October 10am arrival
  Solihull Christadelphian Hall, Grove Ave, Solihull B91 2AH
£  10 for the day

A day of bible study and fellowship for those 25 and over. Both baptised and non baptised are welcome.

Bro. Josh Pearson will do a short introduction to the subject. Then there will be breakout groups which will encourage people to explore this subject for themselves. There will be discussion groups and also opportunity for arts and crafts, music and other things

£10 for the day. Lunch and dinner provided. There will be a range of activities organised by the members of Solihull Ecclesia.

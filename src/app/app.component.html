Solihull Christadelphian 25+ Fellowship Day

EDIFYING

Bro Tim Cresswell

God willing...
  12th November, 10am arrival for drinks with a 10:30 start
  Solihull Christadelphian Hall, Grove Ave, Solihull B91 2AH

A day of bible study and fellowship for those 25 and over. Both baptised and non baptised are welcome.
Bro Tim will be leading a mixture of talks and discussion based on the subject "Edifying". We will focus on the importance of fellowship and how we can encourage each other in our shared faith.
